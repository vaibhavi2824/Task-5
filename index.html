About Us

Maybelline is a well-known American cosmetics brand that was founded in 1915 by Thomas Lyle Williams. The brand's first product was inspired by a homemade mascara mixture used by Williams' sister, Mabel. Over the years, Maybelline has grown significantly and was acquired by L'Or√©al in 1996, becoming part of one of the world's largest beauty companies.

Maybelline's mission is to provide innovative, affordable, and easy-to-use beauty products that inspire confidence and self-expression. Their extensive product range includes foundations, mascaras, lipsticks, nail polishes, and skincare items, making beauty accessible to everyone. Known for its iconic slogan, "Maybe she's born with it. Maybe it's Maybelline," the brand has a strong global presence and is available in over 120 countries.

The brand values inclusivity, innovation, and sustainability. Maybelline strives to cater to diverse skin tones and types, continuously develops new products with the latest technology, and is committed to reducing its environmental impact. Maybelline is also active in empowering individuals and communities through various campaigns and supports charitable causes related to women's rights and health.

For more information, you can visit their official website.
